What is Bruin AI?

Discover Bruin AI, the premier AI consulting club that empowers students to become AI consultants. We provide comprehensive training and real-world experience in the field of artificial intelligence.

Our Mission

Bruin AI at UCLA seeks to drive AI innovation and impact across industries through diverse projects, discussions, and our consultant program, cultivating emerging AI thought leaders

What You Gain

New members will gain AI expertise through tailored hands-on projects or consulting, along with networking and career resources, positioning them at the forefront of the AI revolution.

Bruin AI Workshops

Bruin AI consists of five workshops: Consulting, Design, Engineering, Finance, and Education. Each workshop plays a vital role in our club's mission, enabling members to specialize and contribute to a wide range of AI projects

Team

Meet the Bruin AI Team, our dedicated team of executive members who plan and supervise our projects. They provide leadership and direction, ensuring the success of our initiatives and the growth of our members.
